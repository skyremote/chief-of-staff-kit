## Working style

Practical over hype. Decisive — make the call, state the assumption, move. Scope
tightly: a small thing that ships and proves value beats a broad thing that
impresses and stalls. Commit and price on value delivered, not effort spent. Say no
to bad-fit work, and say *who it's not for* as clearly as who it is for — the honest
filter is part of the offer. Protect the integrity of the domain; surface risk early
and underwrite for the downside.
